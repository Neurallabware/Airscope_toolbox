# Code of Conduct

The Airscope project aims to support reproducible, collaborative scientific
work. Contributors and users are expected to communicate respectfully and focus
on technical, scientific, and reproducibility concerns.

## Expected Behaviour

- Use clear and professional language.
- Give actionable feedback and distinguish evidence from opinion.
- Respect different levels of experience with hardware, firmware, software, and
  neuroscience methods.
- Preserve appropriate attribution for code, data, hardware designs, and
  documentation.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Misrepresenting authorship, provenance, licences, or experimental results.

## Reporting

Report concerns through the issue tracker or by contacting the corresponding
authors listed in the manuscript. Maintainers may edit or remove comments,
close issues, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project
productive and professional.
